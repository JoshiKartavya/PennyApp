# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: June 3, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you install and use the **Penny** mobile application (referred to as the "App"), an expense-tracking and split-billing application.

---

### 1. Information Collection and Use

For a better experience while using our App, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information. The information that we request will be retained by us and used as described in this privacy policy.

The App collects and processes the following types of information:

*   **Account and Authentication Information:** 
    *   **Email Address and Password:** When you register or sign in, we collect your email address and password credentials to create and manage your secure account.
    *   **User Name/Profile Name:** We collect your name to display on your profile and to identify you to other users you connect with.
*   **Transaction and Financial Data:** 
    *   **Wallet Transactions:** We collect and store details of your logged transactions, including the transaction type (income/expense), amount, description, payment method (e.g., online, cash), and timestamp.
    *   **Presets:** We store any transaction templates (presets) you create to help you log expenses faster.
*   **Split Billing and Connection Data:** 
    *   **Connections:** If you choose to use our split billing feature, we collect the email addresses of the friends you connect with. We store the connection status, custom display names, and mutual balances.
    *   **Split Transactions:** When you record shared expenses, we collect and store the split details, such as who paid, who owes, the amount, the description, and the timestamp.
    *   **Notifications:** We store split requests and connection invitations (pending, accepted, or declined status) to enable real-time collaboration.

### 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Your data is shared only under the following conditions:

*   **With Connected Users:** In order for the split-billing feature to work, certain information (such as your profile name, email, mutual balance, and split transaction descriptions/amounts) is shared with the specific users you have explicitly connected with in the App.
*   **Service Providers:** As described above, your authentication and database records are securely hosted and processed by Google/Firebase.
*   **Law Enforcement:** We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency).

We value your trust in providing us your personal information, and we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. The App transmits all data securely over HTTPS (SSL/TLS) to Google Firebase servers. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
